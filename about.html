Welcome to Tosh AutoHub

Established in 2008, Tosh AutoHub has grown from a small local dealership to one of the most reputable premium car retailers in the region. Our journey began with a simple vision: to provide exceptional vehicles and superior customer service.

At Tosh AutoHub, we believe that buying a car should be an exciting and fulfilling experience. Our team of automotive enthusiasts is dedicated to helping you find the perfect vehicle that matches your lifestyle, needs, and budget. We pride ourselves on our transparent approach, honest communication, and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our showroom features a carefully curated selection of premium vehicles, each meticulously inspected and prepared to meet our stringent quality standards. From luxury sedans to high-performance sports cars and family-friendly SUVs, we offer a diverse range of options to cater to different preferences and requirements.

Beyond selling cars, we are committed to building lasting relationships with our customers. Our comprehensive after-sales service ensures that your vehicle remains in optimal condition throughout its lifetime, giving you peace of mind and confidence in your purchase.

Our Journey

2008

The Beginning

Tosh AutoHub was founded by automotive enthusiast James Tosh with a small showroom and a team of three dedicated professionals.

2011

Expansion

After three successful years, we expanded our operations by moving to a larger showroom and increasing our inventory to include more premium brands.

2014

Service Center

We opened our state-of-the-art service center to provide comprehensive maintenance and repair services to our growing customer base.

2018

Industry Recognition

Tosh AutoHub was recognized as one of the top automotive retailers in the region, receiving multiple awards for customer satisfaction and business excellence.

2021

Digital Transformation

We embraced digital technology to enhance the customer experience, launching our online inventory system and virtual showroom tours.

2025

Today

With a team of over 50 professionals and a reputation for excellence, Tosh AutoHub continues to grow while maintaining its commitment to quality and customer service.
